Houston, TX

Why Visit?

Houston is the largest city in Texas and a hub of space exploration, arts, and international cuisine. It’s home to NASA’s Johnson Space Center, a thriving museum district, and a dynamic food scene that reflects the city’s incredible diversity. Whether you’re here for a weekend or a week, there’s something for everyone.

Must-See Attractions

  • Space Center Houston
  • Museuem of Fine Arts
  • Houston Zoo
  • Hermann Park
  • Buffalo Bayou Park
  • The Galleria (shopping)

How to Get There

  1. Fly into George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH) or William P. Hobby Airport (HOU).
  2. User METRO, rideshare apps, or rent a car to get around – Houston is very spread out.
  3. Head to the Museuem District or downtown area to start exploring
  4. Plan ahead for traffic – it's Houston, after all.
