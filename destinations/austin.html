Austin, TX

Why Visit?

Austin is Texas' capital of creativity, live music, and outdoor fun. Known for its "Keep Austin Weird" spirit, the city draws artists, foodies, and nature lovers alike. From Barton Springs to Sixth Street nightlife, it's got a flavor all its own. Come for the tacos, stay for the vibes.

Must-See Attractions

  • Texas State Capitol
  • Barton Springs Pool
  • Zilker Park & Lady Bird Lake
  • The University of Texas campus
  • South Congress Avenue (SoCo)
  • Live music venues on Sixth Street

How to Get There

  1. Fly into Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS)
  2. Grab a rental car, or use local rideshare and scooters to get around
  3. Stay central to enjoy downtown and East Austin with ease
  4. Use public bike paths or kayak rentals to explore Lady Bird Lake
