San Antonio, TX

Why Visit?

San Antonio is a vibrant blend of Texan history and Mexican-American culture. Whether you're strolling the River Walk, exploring centuries-old missions, or enjoying authentic Tex-Mex cuisine, the city offers a perfect mix of charm, culture, and entertainment. It's a must-see for history buffs and families alike.

Must-See Attractions

  • The Alamo
  • San Antonio River Walk
  • Historic Market Square
  • San Antonio Missions National Historical Park
  • Tower of the Americas
  • Natural Bridge Caverns

How to Get There

  1. Book a flight to San Antonio International Airport (SAT)
  2. Upon arrival, take a ride-share or rental car to your hotel
  3. Start your adventure downtown – most attractions are walkable or trolley-accessible
  4. Consider taking a riverboat tour for a relaxing intro to the city
